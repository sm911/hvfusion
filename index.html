What This Is

A meetup for musicians who want to push it

HV Fusion Jam was started by Hudson Valley musicians tired of playing the same I-IV-V changes. We wanted a space to explore odd meters, modal excursions, and the space between rock energy and jazz harmony — without needing to audition or commit to a full band.

Whether you're a working gigging musician or a bedroom shredder who hasn't played with other people in years, this is the room for you. Show up, plug in, and play.

How It Works

No auditions. Just show up and play.

01

RSVP & Show Up

Sign up below so we can plan space and backline. Sessions are held monthly at rotating venues around the Hudson Valley — Poughkeepsie, Kingston, Beacon, and beyond.

02

Meet & Mix

We kick off each session with an intro round — who you are, what you play, what you love. Then the host puts together rotating combos and calls tunes. Lead sheets provided for suggested repertoire.

03

Play, Listen, Grow

Two-hour jam block with rotating lineups. Every combo gets a set. Hang after and connect — this is as much about finding collaborators as it is about the music.

Where We Meet

Hudson Valley, New York

We rotate between venues in the heart of the Hudson Valley — Poughkeepsie, Beacon, Kingston, and New Paltz — keeping the commute short for musicians across Dutchess, Ulster, and Orange counties.

  • Rotating venues: Poughkeepsie · Beacon · Kingston · New Paltz
  • Last Saturday of each month, 6:00 – 9:00 PM
  • Backline provided — drums, bass amp, guitar amp, PA
  • Exact venue shared with confirmed RSVPs one week prior
  • Free to attend — just bring your instrument and an open ear
